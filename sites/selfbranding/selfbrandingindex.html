About
Welcome to my page! My undergraduate degree is in Graphic Design and Art History with a Cultural Ceritage Certification. I'm currently working on a degree in Digital Art History with Duke University. Both of these experiences are referenced in my work. Some projects involve art history, others are more pop culture specific, and some are built solely to client specifications.

The work included in this portfolio showcases both my professional and academic projects. These projects are anything from restaurant branding, digital modelling, logo rebranding, logo creation, and more. The digital models include an undergraduate project modelling the Asklepeion. The other project listed involves digital modelling with Coastal Carolina University working with the Gullah community. The end goal of this project is an education video game used in public schools to teach the history and culture of the Gullah community.
